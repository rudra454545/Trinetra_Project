    ALGORITHM IN ACTION

    Our MLP algorithm uses Trilateration. When an enemy emits a signal, at least three of our jets receive it at slightly different nanoseconds. These tiny time differences are the inputs. The AI processes these inputs through its hidden layers to calculate and predict the enemy's exact coordinates.
